Our Professional Services

We provide comprehensive tree care services to residential and commercial clients throughout Maine.

Tree Trimming & Pruning

Proper pruning enhances tree health, improves appearance, and prevents potential hazards. Our certified arborists use industry best practices.

Tree Removal

Safe and efficient removal of hazardous or unwanted trees. We handle everything from small ornamentals to large, complex removals.

Stump Grinding

Remove unsightly stumps with our professional grinding equipment. We'll leave your yard clean and ready for new landscaping.

Emergency Storm Damage

24/7 emergency response for storm-damaged trees. We prioritize safety and quick response to protect your property.

Tree Health Care

Diagnosis and treatment of tree diseases, pest infestations, and nutrient deficiencies to keep your trees thriving.

Landscaping Services

Complete landscape design and installation, including tree planting, shrubbery, and hardscaping to enhance your property's beauty.
